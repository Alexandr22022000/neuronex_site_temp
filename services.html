Basic cooperation models

Project team

We create a team with the required skills for your project. After that, you control the progress of the project, its timing and budget. This model is well suited when project requirements change frequently.

Hourly payment

The project budget is formed from hours worked. This model is well suited when the project requirements, deadlines and technical specifications are not known.

Fixed budget

The budget and the timing of the project are formed at the planning stage. Our company has all the risks. This model is well suited when the project requirements are known, the technical task is formed.
